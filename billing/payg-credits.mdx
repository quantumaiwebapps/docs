## How It Works
- Rate: $0.85/min
- Packs: $10, $25, $50, $100
- No monthly fee
- Grace buffer: -$5

## Auto-Recharge
1. Billing > Credits > Auto-Recharge
2. Set threshold and amount
3. Auto-charges your card

At -$5 balance, calls blocked until you add credits.
