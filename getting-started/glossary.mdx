| Term | What It Means |
|------|---------------|
| **AI Receptionist** | Your virtual phone answerer — sounds like a real person, available 24/7 |
| **Lead** | Someone who called and gave their information (name, phone, what they need) |
| **Booking / Appointment** | A time slot the AI scheduled for you with a customer |
| **Knowledge Base** | Everything your AI knows about your business — services, prices, hours, FAQs |
| **Call Forwarding** | Redirecting your existing phone calls to your AI number |
| **Routing Mode** | How calls are handled — AI answers all, ring you first, or schedule-based |
| **Automation** | Something that happens automatically, like texting a customer after a missed call |
| **Conversion Rate** | The percentage of calls that turn into leads or bookings |
| **Sentiment** | How the caller felt during the call — positive, neutral, or negative (AI-detected) |
| **Temperature** | How likely a lead is to convert — HOT (very likely), WARM, COOL, COLD (unlikely) |
| **Quote** | A price estimate sent to a customer after a call |
| **Squad / Smart Routing** | Multiple AI specialists handling different parts of a call (booking, pricing, transfer) |
| **IVR** | Interactive Voice Response — menu options offered to callers at the start of a call |
| **SMS Opt-Out** | When a customer texts STOP, they won't receive more texts from your number |
| **TCPA** | US law requiring consent before recording calls — your AI can ask automatically |
| **HIPAA** | Healthcare privacy rules — enable this if you're a medical or dental practice |
| **CRM** | Customer Relationship Management — tools like HubSpot, Salesforce, GoHighLevel |
| **Webhook** | A way for systems to talk to each other automatically — you don't need to know this |
| **VAPI** | The AI voice technology powering your receptionist — you don't need to know this |
| **Twilio** | The phone system powering your AI number — you don't need to know this |
| **Disposition** | The outcome of a call — e.g., NEW_LEAD, BOOKED, TRANSFERRED, FAQ_ANSWERED, SPAM |
| **System Prompt** | The behind-the-scenes instructions that tell your AI how to behave, what to say, and what to know |
| **Squad** | A multi-assistant setup where different AI specialists handle different parts of a call (e.g., one for booking, one for pricing) |
| **Transcript** | The full text version of a phone call, generated automatically by speech-to-text AI |
| **Sentiment** | The AI's assessment of how the caller felt during the call — Positive, Neutral, or Negative |
| **Overage** | Extra minutes used beyond your plan's monthly allowance, billed at a per-minute rate |
| **Provisioning** | The process of getting a new phone number assigned to your account (happens automatically during signup) |
| **Sync** | Pushing your latest settings, Knowledge Base, and configuration to the AI so it uses up-to-date information on calls |
