Your **My Business** page is the foundation of your AI receptionist. Every piece of information here is used by the AI to answer customer questions accurately. The more you fill in, the smarter your AI becomes.

To get here, click **My Business** in the left sidebar.

---

## Business Details

### Business Name

The name your AI uses when answering calls ("Thanks for calling **Smith Plumbing**, how can I help?"). Type your business name exactly as you want it spoken aloud.

### Website

Your business website URL (e.g. `https://www.smithplumbing.co.uk`). This is used for:
- The initial AI setup (we scan your website to pre-fill services, hours, and FAQs)
- The **Re-scan Website** button (more on that below)

### Description

A free-text area where you describe your business in your own words. Write 2-4 sentences about what you do, who you serve, and what makes you different. The AI uses this as context when speaking to callers.

**Example:** "Smith Plumbing is a family-run plumbing company based in Manchester. We handle everything from leaky taps to full bathroom refits. We've been trading for 15 years and all our plumbers are Gas Safe registered."

## Services

A **tag input** field where you list every service you offer.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Type a service name">
    Click into the field and type a service (e.g. "Boiler Repair").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Press Enter to add">
    The service appears as a tag/chip in the field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add as many as you need">
    Keep typing and pressing Enter. Common examples: "Emergency Callout", "Bathroom Installation", "Drain Unblocking", "Annual Boiler Service".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove a service">
    Click the **X** on any tag to remove it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Be specific. Instead of "Plumbing", add "Boiler Repair", "Leak Repair", "Bathroom Fitting", "Drain Unblocking" separately. This helps the AI answer questions like "Do you fix boilers?" with confidence.
</Tip>

---

## Qualifications and Certifications

Another **tag input** field. Add any professional qualifications, certifications, or accreditations your business holds.

**Examples:** "Gas Safe Registered", "NICEIC Approved", "City & Guilds Level 3", "Which? Trusted Trader", "Checkatrade Verified", "FGAS Certified".

The AI will mention these when callers ask "Are you qualified?" or "Are you registered?"

## Business Hours

A **7-day grid** showing your opening hours for each day of the week (Monday through Sunday).

<Steps>
  <Step title="Toggle each day">
    Each day has an **on/off toggle** on the left. Turn it on for days you are open, off for days you are closed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set start and end times">
    For each active day, use the two time pickers to set your opening time and closing time. Click the time field and select from the dropdown, or type directly (e.g. "09:00" and "17:30").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open 24/7 toggle">
    If your business operates around the clock, toggle on **Open 24/7** at the top of the hours section. This overrides all individual day settings and tells the AI you are always available.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
These hours are used by the AI when answering "What time do you open?" and when checking availability for bookings (if no calendar is connected). Keep them accurate.
</Info>

## Pricing

Add your service pricing so the AI can give callers accurate price information.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Add Pricing">
    A new pricing row appears.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the service name">
    Type the name of the service (e.g. "Boiler Service").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose pricing type">
    Select from the **dropdown**:
    - **Fixed** — A set price (e.g. "80")
    - **Starting From** — A minimum price (e.g. "From 80")
    - **Hourly** — An hourly rate (e.g. "60/hour")
    - **Free** — No charge (e.g. for initial consultations)
    - **Quote Required** — The AI will tell callers "Pricing depends on the job, we'll provide a quote"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the amount">
    Type the price number. For "Quote Required" and "Free", this field is not shown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select currency">
    Choose **GBP** or **USD** from the currency dropdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add more rows or remove">
    Click **Add Pricing** again for additional services. Click the **trash icon** on any row to remove it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Even if your pricing varies, adding rough figures helps. "Starting From 60" is better than nothing — the AI can say "Our callout charge starts from 60 pounds, but the exact price depends on the job."
</Tip>

## Payment Methods

A **tag input** field listing how customers can pay you. Type each method and press Enter.

**Common options:** Cash, Card, Bank Transfer, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Cheque, Finance Available.

The AI uses this when callers ask "Do you take card?" or "Can I pay by bank transfer?"

---

## Delivery Mode

A **dropdown** with three options:

- **In-Person** — You go to the customer or they come to you (most tradespeople, salons, dental practices)
- **Remote** — You work remotely (consultants, IT support)
- **Both** — You offer both in-person and remote services

## Representative

- **Name** — The name of the person who represents the business (usually the owner). This may be used in email signatures and AI responses.
- **Title** — Their job title (e.g. "Owner", "Managing Director", "Head Stylist").

---

## Additional Details

- **Year Founded** — The year your business started. The AI can mention this when callers ask how long you have been trading.
- **Coverage Distance** — How far you are willing to travel (e.g. "20 miles", "Greater Manchester area"). The AI uses this to let callers know if they are within your service area.

## Intelligence Score

At the top right of the My Business page, you will see an **Intelligence Score** card. This shows how well-informed your AI receptionist is, on a scale of 0 to 100.

### What the score means

| Score | Rating | What it means |
|-------|--------|---------------|
| 80-100 | Excellent | Your AI can answer almost any question confidently. |
| 60-79 | Good | Your AI handles most calls well but has some gaps. |
| 40-59 | Fair | Callers may get vague answers on some topics. |
| 0-39 | Needs Work | Your AI is missing key information. Fill in more fields. |

### Category breakdown

Below the score, you will see a breakdown by category:
- **Services** (20% weight) — How well your services are documented
- **Hours** (15%) — Whether your opening hours are set
- **Pricing** (15%) — How many services have pricing information
- **Location** (10%) — Whether your address and coverage area are set
- **FAQs** (10%) — How many frequently asked questions are answered in your Knowledge Base
- **About** (10%) — Business description, qualifications, year founded
- **Contact** (10%) — Phone, email, social media
- **Policies** (10%) — Cancellation policy, warranty info, payment terms

### Re-scan Website Button

Click **Re-scan Website** to have the system crawl your website again and pull in any new or updated information. This is useful if you have recently updated your website with new services, pricing, or opening hours.

<Warning>
Re-scanning will not overwrite any fields you have manually edited. It only fills in fields that are currently empty. Your manual changes are always preserved.
</Warning>

## Saving

Click the **Save** button at the bottom of the page to save all your changes. A green notification will confirm your changes were saved successfully.

<Tip>
After saving, your AI receptionist uses the updated information immediately on the next call. There is no separate sync step needed for business profile changes — they go live as soon as you save.
</Tip>
