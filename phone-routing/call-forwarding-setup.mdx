## What is call forwarding?

Call forwarding sends calls from your existing phone number to your AI receptionist when you do not answer (or always). This means you can keep your current number — customers dial the same number they always have, and the AI picks up when you are busy.

<Tip>
The easiest setup is "forward when unanswered." Your phone rings for 15 to 20 seconds, and if you do not pick up, the call goes to your AI receptionist. You never miss a call again.
</Tip>

## Before you start

You will need:
1. **Your AI phone number** — find it on the **Phone** page in your dashboard
2. **Your mobile phone** — to dial the forwarding codes
3. **2 minutes** — that is all it takes

## Forward types

Most carriers support three types of forwarding:

| Type | When it forwards | Best for |
|------|-----------------|----------|
| **When unanswered** | After your phone rings for 15-20 seconds | Most businesses — you try to answer first, AI catches what you miss |
| **All calls** | Every call goes straight to AI | When you are on holiday, in meetings, or want AI to handle everything |
| **When busy** | Only when you are already on another call | Businesses that get lots of simultaneous calls |

<Info>
We recommend "when unanswered" for most businesses. This gives you the chance to answer personally, with the AI as your safety net.
</Info>

## In-app instructions with exact dial codes

Your dashboard has personalised instructions with your AI number already filled in — no need to type it yourself.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Phone">
    Click **Phone** in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scroll to Call Forwarding">
    Scroll down to the **Call Forwarding** section. You will see carrier cards for your country.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click your carrier">
    Click the card for your phone carrier. It expands to show step-by-step instructions with your AI number pre-filled in the dial codes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy and dial">
    Click the **Copy** button next to any dial code to copy it to your clipboard, then paste it into your phone's dialler.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Some carriers charge a small fee for call forwarding. Check with your carrier before enabling. Most UK and US carriers include it for free on contract plans, but pay-as-you-go plans may charge per forwarded minute.
</Warning>

## Testing that forwarding works

After setting up forwarding:

1. Call your existing business number from a different phone
2. Let it ring without answering (for "unanswered" forwarding)
3. Your AI receptionist should pick up after 15-20 seconds
4. If it does not work, try the cancel code first, then re-enter the forwarding code

<Tip>
If forwarding does not activate, your carrier may require you to enable it through their app or by calling customer service. This is common with some business phone plans.
</Tip>
