## How Agency Billing Works

As an agency, you pay a monthly subscription for the platform, and your clients pay you for AI receptionist minutes. You set your own prices and keep the margin.

```
Client deposits $100 → 5% platform fee ($5) → $95 to your Stripe balance
```

---

## Stripe Connect

Stripe Connect is how you receive client payments. You must connect a Stripe account before clients can deposit funds.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Agency Settings > Billing">
    Click **Connect Stripe Account** to start the Stripe Connect onboarding flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Stripe onboarding">
    Stripe will ask for your business details, bank account, and identity verification. This is a one-time process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify connection">
    Once complete, the Billing page shows your Stripe Connect status as "Connected" with your account ID.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>Clients cannot deposit funds or auto-recharge until your Stripe Connect account is fully verified. Complete this step before onboarding clients.</Warning>

## Client Wallet

Each client has a prepaid wallet that funds their AI call minutes.

### How the wallet works:

- **Deposits** — clients add funds via Stripe (charged to their card, transferred to your Stripe account minus the 5% platform fee)
- **Auto-recharge** — clients can set a threshold and reload amount (e.g., "Add $50 when balance drops below $10")
- **Per-minute deduction** — each AI call minute deducts from the wallet at the rate you set
- **Transaction history** — every deposit, deduction, and credit is logged

### Credits vs Balance

| Type | Source | Priority | Refundable? |
|------|--------|----------|-------------|
| **Credits** | Gifted by agency (promotional, onboarding bonus) | Used first | No |
| **Balance** | Deposited by client via Stripe | Used after credits | Yes (via Stripe) |

<Tip>Gift new clients 30 minutes of credits as an onboarding incentive. It costs you nothing (credits are not charged to Stripe) and helps them see value immediately.</Tip>

## Per-Minute Pricing

You control what you charge clients per minute. Set this in **Agency Settings > Pricing**.

**Example margin calculation:**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| VAPI cost to platform | ~$0.05/min |
| Platform charges agency | $0.07/min |
| Agency charges client | $0.15/min |
| **Your margin** | **$0.08/min** |

<Info>The platform cost varies by voice provider and call duration. Check your VAPI dashboard for exact per-minute rates. Most agencies charge 2-3x the platform cost.</Info>

## Revenue Dashboard

Navigate to **Agency Portal > Analytics** to see your revenue metrics:

- **MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)** — total expected revenue this month based on active clients
- **Total Revenue** — lifetime revenue across all clients
- **Per-client breakdown** — table showing each client's deposits, usage, and balance
- **Revenue trend chart** — 6-month chart of monthly revenue

### Downloading Reports

Click **Download CSV** on the analytics page to export:
- Client name, email, plan
- Total deposited, total used, current balance
- Call count, lead count, appointment count
- Health score and temperature

## Platform Fee

The platform charges a **5% fee** on all client deposits. This is deducted automatically before funds reach your Stripe balance.

| Client Deposits | Platform Fee (5%) | You Receive |
|----------------|-------------------|-------------|
| $50 | $2.50 | $47.50 |
| $100 | $5.00 | $95.00 |
| $500 | $25.00 | $475.00 |

<Info>The platform fee covers infrastructure costs (VAPI, Twilio, hosting). Your monthly agency subscription covers the dashboard, branding, and support.</Info>

## How Payouts Work

Stripe Connect handles payouts to your bank account:

1. Client deposits funds via Stripe
2. 5% platform fee is deducted
3. Remaining amount lands in your Stripe Connect balance
4. Stripe pays out to your bank on your configured schedule (daily, weekly, or monthly)

You can check payout status and history directly in your Stripe dashboard at [dashboard.stripe.com](https://dashboard.stripe.com).

## Client Invoices

Clients can download invoices for their deposits from their own dashboard under **Billing > Transaction History**. Each transaction shows:

- Date and amount
- Payment method (last 4 digits)
- Invoice number
- Download link (PDF)

<Accordion title="Can I generate invoices for clients manually?">
  Yes. Navigate to a client's detail page via impersonation and go to **Billing > Invoices**. You can create a custom invoice with a due date and send it via email with a Stripe payment link.
</Accordion>
