## Setup

1. Go to **Integrations**
2. Click **Connect with Meta** under Facebook Messenger
3. Select your Facebook Business Page
4. AI starts replying instantly

## How It Works

Visitor messages your Page, AI replies in seconds using your knowledge base. FREE - no per-message charges.

## FAQ

**Need a Facebook Page?** Yes.
**Can I reply manually?** Yes, from the Conversations page.
