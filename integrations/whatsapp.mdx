## Two Setup Options

### Option 1: Instant Setup (Recommended)
We provide you a dedicated WhatsApp number. Zero configuration needed.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Integrations">Navigate to Integrations in your dashboard</Step>
  <Step title="Click Instant Setup">Under WhatsApp, select "Instant Setup"</Step>
  <Step title="Done">Your AI WhatsApp number is ready. Share it with customers.</Step>
</Steps>

### Option 2: Use Your Own Number (BYON)
Connect your existing WhatsApp Business number via Meta.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Use My Number">Under WhatsApp, select "Use My Number"</Step>
  <Step title="Authorize Meta">Log into Facebook and grant access to your WhatsApp Business account</Step>
  <Step title="Auto-discovery">We automatically find your WhatsApp number, Facebook Page, and Instagram account</Step>
  <Step title="AI starts replying">Messages to your WhatsApp number get AI replies instantly</Step>
</Steps>

## How It Works

1. Customer sends WhatsApp message to your number
2. Our webhook receives the message instantly
3. AI processes it using your knowledge base (same brain as phone calls)
4. AI reply sent back via WhatsApp within seconds
5. Conversation appears in your dashboard under Conversations → WhatsApp tab
