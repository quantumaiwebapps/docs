## What Are Campaigns?

Campaigns let your AI receptionist make outbound calls to a list of contacts automatically. Instead of you picking up the phone to chase leads or ask for reviews, your AI does it for you.

Common uses:
- **Appointment reminders** — reduce no-shows
- **Review requests** — ask happy customers to leave a Google review
- **Special offers** — let past customers know about a promotion
- **Lead follow-ups** — call back leads who enquired but did not book

<Info>
  Outbound campaign calls use your included plan minutes, just like inbound calls.
</Info>

## Creating a Campaign

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Campaigns">
    Click **Campaigns** in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click New Campaign">
    Click the **New Campaign** button in the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the details">
    - **Name** — a label for your reference (e.g. "March Review Request")
    - **Purpose** — choose from Reminder, Review Request, Special Offer, or Other
    - **Select contacts** — pick from your existing leads and customers, or add phone numbers manually
    - **Schedule** — choose when calls should go out (date, time window). Calls are only made during business hours.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and save">
    Check the summary. Your campaign is saved as a draft until you launch it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Launching a Campaign

From the Campaigns page, find your draft campaign and click **Launch**. Your AI will start calling contacts at the scheduled time.

<Warning>
  Once launched, calls will begin at the scheduled time and cannot be undone for contacts already called. You can cancel remaining calls at any time.
</Warning>

## Tracking Results

Each campaign shows:
- **Total contacts** — how many people are in the campaign
- **Completed** — calls that connected and finished
- **No answer** — calls that went to voicemail or were not picked up
- **Pending** — calls that have not been made yet

Click into any campaign to see per-contact results, including call duration and outcome.

## Cancelling a Campaign

Click into the campaign and press **Cancel Campaign**. This stops all remaining calls that have not been made yet. Calls already completed are not affected.

<Tip>
  Start with a small test campaign (5-10 contacts) before running a large one. This lets you check the AI sounds right for outbound calls.
</Tip>

## Limits

- Campaigns only call during your business hours
- Maximum **5 outbound calls per day** per contact (across all campaigns)
- Outbound calls count toward your plan's included minutes
